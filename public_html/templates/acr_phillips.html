ACR® RIBBED PHILLIPS® CRUCIFORM DRIVE SYSTEM

Eliminates fastener recess damage and drill outs.

The ACR® Ribbed PHILLIPS® Cruciform Drive System advanced design keeps the driver securely mated to the screw during fastener removal, eliminating recess damage and damage to surrounding areas. The results are securely removed screws every time with lower costs, more productive workers and fewer drill outs.

SIZES

0 thru 6 (0 thru 1"
diameter fasteners)

BENEFITS

ACR ribs

  • Improved removal without cam-out
  • Off-angle driving ability
  • Interlocks in removal requiring lower end load
  • Reduces worker fatigue
  • Reduces scarring surrounding panels

Compatible with common
Phillips drivers

  • ACR Phillips ribbed cruciform screws can be removed or installed in an emergency with common tools

Slanted rib design in recess

  • Better rib form in all materials
  • Higher heading speed in manufacturing

Angled recess side walls

  • Allows off-angle driving
  • Acts as torque limit on installation

PSC quality inspections

  • Assures global system quality and compatibility
